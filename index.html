About PBSMUN

Welcome to the Pace British School Model United Nations (PBSMUN) 2026. We are dedicated to providing a platform for the leaders of tomorrow to engage in meaningful diplomatic debate, hone their public speaking skills, and tackle the world's most pressing issues.


At PBSMUN, we believe in the power of youth to drive global change. Join us this September as we step into the shoes of global policymakers.

Our Mission

PBSMUN '26 aims to create an environment where diplomacy meets innovation. Our mission is to empower students with the research, negotiation, and leadership skills necessary to navigate a globalized world.


We pride ourselves on high-quality academic standards and a welcoming atmosphere for both first-time delegates and experienced veterans. Our chairs are dedicated to fostering fair, inclusive, and rigorous debate across all committees.
