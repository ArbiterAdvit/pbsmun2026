Meet The Team

ABOUT US

Pace British School aims to offer an inclusive opportunity for growth alongside a respectful display of skill, creativity and ambition with the aid of our dedicated staff and brilliant students.


We at PBS MUN believe in the power of the youth to drive positive change in the world. As such, PBSMUN made to be a place where students can display and develop their skills, while simultaneously working towards developing solutions to change this world for the better. Debates, POIs, speeches, challenges, resolution papers- all aspects of an MUN—are integrated to create an engaging, educational, and impactful experience for every delegate.

MEET OUR SECRETARIAT

Welcome from the Secretary-General

I am delighted to welcome you all to our first ever PACE British School Model United Nations Conference (PBSMUN). Being your Secretary-General for this conference is more than just a title to me, it is a responsibility and a chance I truly appreciate.

I used to sit nervously in committee sessions as a delegate, and now I am leading this conference. I’ve seen firsthand just how powerful MUN's can be. It is about more than just speeches and resolutions. It is a place where people start to build confidence, question their views, make friends, and leaders begin to find their voice. MUN has changed how I see things, how I talk, and how I understand what is going on around me, and I hope this conference has the same impact on you.

We live in a time where people are more connected than ever, but somehow, that connection does not always bring us together like it should. Problems around the world do not stop at borders, and finding answers means working together. Each of you stands for a country, a perspective, and a duty. During this conference, you will spend time debating, negotiating, making compromises, and working together. Along the way, you will see that diplomacy isn’t just about winning debates but about creating an understanding environment.

This conference is a place not only to talk but to listen, not just to compete but to learn and grow. If this is your first time as a delegate or you have done it before, I invite you to push yourself a little, speak up with confidence, and really get involved. The difference you make here might be bigger than you think.

Our MUN trusts that young leaders can make a difference. We believe that change does not start in big rooms filled with power, it starts with people talking, sharing ideas, and those who have the courage to think in new ways. Over the next few sessions, I hope you push yourself a bit, encourage each other, and above all, have a great time along the way.

On behalf of the whole Secretariat, I am contented to welcome you to PBSMUN 2026, where ideas come to life and future leaders start their journey.

I am eager to see the diplomacy, passion, and brilliance you all will bring to each committee.


Warm regards,

Huzaifa Anwar
Secretary General, PBSMUN 2026

More About Us

About Pace British School

Opened in 2019, Pace British School is a Cambridge International Curriculum School, alongside offering Edexcel IGCSE’s in Years 10 and 11. The school focuses on academic excellence while encouraging growth and development within a robust and inclusive system made to foster learners for a bright future.

Our History

The first iteration of PBSMUN hopes to support the founder of the PACE Group, Dr. PA Ibrahim Haji's belief that education should not merely impart knowledge but translate it into actions that benefit humanity. Three years ago, the Pace British Lunchtime Club was founded by the Head-boy and the Head-girl at that time, the MUN lunchtime club was comprised of many beginners and the lunchtime club served to help students prepair for their first MUN elsewhere.

Our Experience

Our student-led staff has experience many aspects of MUNs, ranging from delegates with many Best Delegate, Best Research, Best Orator and Honourable Mention Awards, staff with experience as chairs and experience in organizing other events within and outside Pace British School, such as our Bake Sale and Sports day, alongside student leadership roles such as headboy and headgirl, prefect and student council.

Our Mission

The first iteration of PBSMUN aims to grant students a chance to develop their organization skills as they overcome many adversities to bring everyone a welcoming space for growth whilst being a space to appreciate, encourage and develop our core values, which entail being ambitious, internationally minded, inclusive and kind, environmentally responsible alongside the Islamic Values.
