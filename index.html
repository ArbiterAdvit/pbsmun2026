Meet The Team

ABOUT US

Pace British School aims to offer an inclusive opportunity for growth alongside a respectful display of skill, creativity and ambition with the aid of our dedicated staff and brilliant students.


We at PBS MUN believe in the power of the youth to drive positive change in the world. As such, PBSMUN made to be a place where students can display and develop their skills, while simultaneously working towards developing solutions to change this world for the better. Debates, POIs, speeches, challenges, resolution papers- all aspects of an MUN—are integrated to create an engaging, educational, and impactful experience for every delegate.
