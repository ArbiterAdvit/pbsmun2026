Internal Staff (PBS Students)

Are you a Pace British School student looking to help shape the next great MUN conference? We are recruiting for our logistics, media, and operations teams.

What you need to bring:

  • Motivation: A strong drive to contribute to the success of our school's conference.
  • Professionalism: You represent the face of PBSMUN to all visiting delegates.
  • Dedication: Commitment to attending all preparatory meetings and both days of the event.
  • Teamwork: Ability to collaborate across different departments under pressure.

Please note: After submission, our team will review your application. Successful candidates will be waitlisted and contacted via school email for an interview.
