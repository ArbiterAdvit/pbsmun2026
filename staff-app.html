External Staff

At this time, PBSMUN '26 is not accepting applications for external staff members. We appreciate the interest from the wider MUN community and encourage you to apply as a delegate when applications open.

Open for Internal Students

Internal Staff (Pace British School)

We are currently looking for dedicated PBS students to join our logistics, media, and administrative teams.

What we look for:

  • Motivation: A genuine drive to ensure the conference runs smoothly.
  • Reliability: Staff members are the backbone of the event; punctuality and commitment are non-negotiable.
  • Problem Solving: The ability to stay calm under pressure and help delegates with their needs.
  • Teamwork: Collaborating with other departments to create a professional environment.

Note: After submitting your application, our team will review your responses. Successful applicants will be waitlisted for an interview. Please note that submission does not guarantee a position.
